Collage Romance

"College Romance" is an Indian web series created by The Viral Fever (TVF). It follows the lives of three college students - Karan, Trippy, and Naira - as they navigate through the ups and downs of college life, friendships, and relationships. Set in the backdrop of a fictional college named "DU," the series explores themes of love, friendship, ambition, and personal growth. Each episode delves into the various challenges and experiences faced by the characters, including romantic entanglements, academic pressures, and the pursuit of dreams. With its relatable storyline, humor, and engaging characters, "College Romance" has resonated with audiences, particularly young adults, and has garnered a dedicated fan base since its release on YouTube and TVF Play.

Mirzapure

"Mirzapur" is an Indian crime thriller web series created by Puneet Krishna and directed by Karan Anshuman and Gurmmeet Singh. Set in the lawless town of Mirzapur in the Indian state of Uttar Pradesh, the series revolves around the intertwined lives of various characters involved in the world of crime, power, and politics.
